About Me

Hey there, I'm Nidhi Srinath, a Data Scientist with a passion for crafting actionable insights from complex data. With a Bachelor's in Information Science and Engineering and a Master's in Computer Science, I've spent over four years delving deep into the world of data science.
Known for my talent in storytelling with data and expertise in data scraping, cleaning, and analysis, I thrive on unraveling the mysteries within datasets. My proficiency spans descriptive analytics, machine learning, and data visualization, allowing me to transform raw data into strategic insights that drive impactful decisions.
